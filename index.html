Timeline of my journey

Explore anime, magic, beauty, and futuristic themes in art.

I enjoy drawing myself as an anime character and bringing these imaginative themes to life. These genres fuel my creativity and reflect my passion for storytelling through art.

Influences come from the Bible, anime, manga, and music videos.

My creative inspirations are deeply rooted in a mix of spirituality and art. The words of God from the Bible guide my values, while anime, manga, and music videos enrich my imagination. Together, they shape my unique artistic perspective and drive my passion for expression.

Family, cats, nature, and kareshi are my greatest inspirations.

The people and things closest to me inspire my art and creativity. My family’s love, my cats' playful antics, and the beauty of nature remind me of the blessings that God has given me. My kareshi’s support adds another layer of motivation, making every piece of art more meaningful.

From scribbling at age 2 to winning over 20 art competitions, dedication and growth.

I began scribbling as early as two years old, discovering my love for art at a young age. Through the years, I participated in numerous art contests from elementary school to college, earning over 50+ certificates and almost 20 champion titles. Each step of this journey reflects my perseverance and passion for creating art.
